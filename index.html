Batman

About Batman

A rich super-hero that lives in Gotham City. He is a good guy who has access to advanced technology and fights crime during the night.

Batman's Characteristics

  1. He's middle aged rich man fighting crime in the streets of Gotham City.
  2. He only goes out at night to remain hidden in the shadows.
  3. Has a batcave where he can plan his outtings.

This is Batman in action.

Batman's Friends


  • Robin is one of his best friends.


  • Catwoman is a friend/foe.


  • Alfred is his trusting butler.
